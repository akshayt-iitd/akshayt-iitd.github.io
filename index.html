About me

At the forefront of academia, my journey as a Research Scholar at IIT Delhi, enhances my understanding of Operations and Supply Chain Management. Previously, as an Academic Associate at IIM Indore and an Assistant Professor at IIST, I honed my experience in Operations Management and Quantitative Techniques. With a solid foundation from my Master's in Industrial Engineering and Management, my competencies lie in streamlining processes, sustainability and optimizing supply chains. I am looking forward to advancing the field through rigorous research and practical application, driving innovation to address complex challenges in supply chain management.
